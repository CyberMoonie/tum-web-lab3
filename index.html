About Me

I'm Sergiu Munteanu, a photographer driven by the power of light and shadow. My work focuses on black & white imagery — stripping away color to reveal the raw emotion and timeless beauty in every frame.

From intimate wedding moments to vibrant event atmospheres, I document stories that deserve to be remembered. Every photograph I take is crafted with intention, patience, and a deep respect for the subject.
